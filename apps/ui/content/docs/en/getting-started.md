# Getting Started

Welcome to the Athelas documentation. This guide will help you get familiar with the main features of the system.

## Navigation

Use the sidebar to access the different sections:

- **Encounters**: Manage medical encounters with your patients
- **Studies**: Create and manage laboratory studies
- **Patients**: Search and register patients
- **Prescriptions**: Issue prescriptions and medical orders
- **Accounting**: Track billing by insurer
- **Users**: Manage your organization's members
- **Stats**: View metrics on your activity

## Guided Tour

Each section includes a guided tour that starts automatically on your first visit. To replay it, click the help button (?) in the sidebar and select "Start tour".

## Initial Setup

Before getting started, we recommend:

1. Complete your profile in **Settings > Profile**
2. Verify your identity in **Settings > ID Verification** (doctors)
3. Link your WhatsApp account in **Settings > WhatsApp** (administrators)
4. Set up your practices in **Settings > Practices**
